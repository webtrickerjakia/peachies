The Science

Kinder to the planet

We’re always striving to make our products more environmentally friendly, working hard to make smart decisions to reduce the impact of nappies on our planet. The European Union’s climate innovation organisation  (Climate KIC) verified that we will save the equivalent of 93 tonnes of CO2 for every 1,000 babies that wear Peachies. That’s the weight of 17 elephants.

Supporting families

IPeachies is proud to support Save the Children, helping to ensure that every child gets the start to life they deserve. We’re especially passionate about the first 1,000 days parents share with their little ones; from the early days of pregnancy, to the slightly chaotic potty training phase. This period is among the most important in a child’s development. The quality of nutrition, sleep, environment and hygiene for child and parent can swing the pendulum for the child’s IQ a whopping 12 points.
